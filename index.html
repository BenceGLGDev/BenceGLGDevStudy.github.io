Study Tips

Study Techniques

  • Pomodoro Technique: Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break.
  • Feynman Technique: Teach what you’ve learned to someone else.
  • Active Recall: Test yourself on the material instead of just re-reading.
  • Mind Mapping: Visualize concepts and their relationships with diagrams.
